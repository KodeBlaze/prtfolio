Assistive Device for Visually Challenged

A device called Blind Aid is made to help people who are visually impaired navigate their surroundings. Users receive real-time aural input on their surroundings, including knowledge of adjacent impediments, using a mix of hardware and software. The system’s hardware comprises of a compact, portable device with sensors that track the user’s surroundings and evaluate them, as well as a speaker for audio output. Users receive a clear and succinct audio depiction of their surroundings because to the software component’s usage of machine learning algorithms to understand and process the sensor data. Blind Aid helps to enhance the independence and quality of life of visually impaired people by enabling them to more simply and confidently navigate their surroundings.

House Rental Management System

This mobile application is a simple and user-friendly platform designed to help people find rental properties with ease. It allows users to browse available listings, view basic property details such as location, rent, and contact information, and directly connect with property owners. The app focuses on providing a straightforward experience without the involvement of brokers, making the rental process more accessible and affordable. With essential features like property search, listing uploads, and direct messaging, the app serves as a convenient tool for both tenants and landlords in managing rental needs.
